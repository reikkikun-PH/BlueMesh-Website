How It Works

Simple, Powerful, Off-Grid

A few solar-powered signal boxes and the BlueMesh app are all it takes to keep your neighborhood connected.

01

Set Up Solar Boosters

We deploy solar-powered support hardware nodes on roofs or poles across the barangay. These nodes run 24/7 to boost the signal, extending the range of direct phone-to-phone communication.

02

Get the App

Download the free BlueMesh app on your Android phone. Simply enter a display name and set your local lock passcode — no registration or phone number required.

03

Find Your Neighbors

Open the app to automatically see neighbors and solar boxes within range. You are instantly ready to connect.

04

Start Chatting

Type your message and send it! It will securely bounce from neighbor to neighbor, reaching across the community even without cell service.

App Guide

Explore the BlueMesh Interface

Interact with the live guide cards below to see how each core offline feature of the BlueMesh application works.

01

Private & Secure Lock

Keeps your messages locked safely on your phone and out of the wrong hands.

  • End-to-End Encryption: Optional secure chat that locks your messages so that only you and your chat partner can read them—no middleman can intercept them.
  • Private Local Storage: Your chat history is stored only on your own phone and locked behind your passcode, never sent to the internet.
  • Auto-Lock Protection: The app automatically locks itself if left idle for 5 minutes, protecting your privacy if you leave your phone unattended.
  • No Internet Required: Your lock passcode is checked entirely on your phone. No logins, accounts, or internet connection ever needed.
AES-GCM-128 Passcode Lock
02

Neighbor Finder

Finds nearby active neighbors and maps signal paths without internet connection.

  • Smart Battery Saving: Intelligently runs Bluetooth in the background so it doesn't drain your phone's battery.
  • Instant Neighbor Search: Automatically searches and finds nearby friends and community solar boxes without setup.
  • Distance Indicator: Displays a helpful estimate of how close your neighbors are based on their signal.
  • Direct Phone-to-Phone Connection: Connects your phone directly to another device or solar booster for reliable delivery.
BLE GATT Battery Saved
03

Offline Messaging

Compresses and sends text messages safely through other phones in the area.

  • Instant Compression: Automatically shrinks text size so messages travel much faster over Bluetooth channels.
  • Automatic Message Delivery: If your friend is too far away, the app saves your message and sends it automatically the instant they come back in range.
  • Smart Splitting: Splits long messages into smaller parts so they travel reliably across the network without failing.
  • Signal Hopping: Messages can jump from phone to phone, using neighboring devices to reach people further away.
LZ4 Compress SQLite Queue Dynamic MTU
04

Location Distance Sharing

Voluntarily share your estimated distance to let others know if you are nearby.

  • Opt-In Distance Sharing: Choose whether to make your estimated distance visible to other nearby users on the network.
  • Signal-Based Estimation: Estimates your distance relative to neighbors using Bluetooth signal strength (RSSI).
  • Completely Offline: Works entirely without GPS satellites or cell towers, relying only on direct Bluetooth signal checks.
  • Support Node Routing: Automatically passes your proximity status through solar-powered relay nodes to extend the signal range across the barangay.
Distance Share BLE RSSI
About the Project

Communication for Every Barangay

BlueMesh is an open‑source, direct phone‑to‑phone messaging app designed for local communities. It lets you send text messages entirely without cellular load, internet, or WiFi, utilizing solar‑powered relay nodes as range-extending support hardware across the neighborhood.

It features optional End-to-End Encryption (E2EE), easy distance indicators, optional estimated distance sharing, a secure app passcode lock, background battery optimization, and dynamic text compression for fast, reliable delivery.

  • Works without cell towers or power grids
  • 100% Free — no SIM card, load, or data needed
  • Completely Private — your data stays on your phone
  • Disaster-Resilient — built for typhoons and emergencies
